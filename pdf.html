    How it works

    Pick an operation, add your files in the order you want, then click “Create PDF”. The new file is built right in your browser and downloads instantly — your documents never leave your device.

    Tips

    • Merge PDF: Files are combined in the order shown. Add them in the order you want.
    • Images to PDF: Each image becomes one page. JPG and PNG are supported.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Are my documents uploaded?

    No. The merging happens entirely on your device, inside your browser.

    Is there a limit?

    No artificial limit — only what your device's memory can handle.

    Is it free?

    Yes, completely free and without watermarks.
