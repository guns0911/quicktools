About

QuickTools is a project offering free, private online tools that speed up your everyday digital tasks — from compressing images to generating QR codes and merging PDFs.

Why QuickTools?

  • Privacy first: Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded.
  • Free: No sign-up, no hidden fees, no limits.
  • Fast: Everything happens instantly in your browser.
  • Any device: Works on desktop, tablet and phone.

Tools available now

  • Image Compressor — compress, resize and convert images.
  • QR Code Generator — create QR codes from text or links.
  • PDF Tools — merge PDFs and convert images to PDF.

Text tools, color utilities and more handy tools are on the way.
