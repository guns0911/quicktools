How to make a QR code

Type or paste your text or link above. The QR code updates automatically. Pick your colors and size, then download it as a PNG image or a scalable SVG. Everything is generated in your browser — nothing is sent to a server.

What can I put in a QR code?

  • A website link for posters, menus or business cards.
  • Plain text, a phone number or an email address.
  • Wi-Fi or contact details in the right text format.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do the QR codes expire?

No. These are static QR codes — they work forever and contain the data directly.

Is it free for commercial use?

Yes. The QR codes you create are yours to use anywhere.

Which format should I download?

Use PNG for quick sharing, SVG for print or large sizes (it stays sharp at any scale).
