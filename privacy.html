Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Where do your images go?

Nowhere. All image processing on QuickTools (compression, resizing, format conversion) happens entirely in your browser, on your own device. The files you upload are never sent to our servers or any third party, and they are not stored or saved anywhere.

We do not collect your personal data. No account or sign-up is required. We may use anonymous visit statistics (such as page views) to improve the site.
